# Release 2.1

**Released:** 2026-04-07
**Tag:** [2.1](https://github.com/nikpetrovic/move-my-mouse-to-hide/releases/tag/2.1)
**Asset:** `com.microsoft.teams.activity.jar`

---

Ever wanted to sneak out of the office — physically or spiritually — but still look like you're grinding away at your desk? **MS Teams Worker v2.1** has your back, your alibi, and your coffee order.

Introducing **Deferred Stop**: set it, forget it, and go live your life. The mover will keep up the charade for exactly as long as you tell it to, then quietly retire — no drama, no traces, just a clean exit like a professional ghost.

### What's new

- ⏳ **Deferred stop** — check the box, enter how many seconds you need covered, and the mover will automatically stop when your time is up. Perfect for "I just stepped away for a second" that somehow lasts two hours.
- 🔢 **Default pause interval bumped to 30 sec** — because 10 seconds was making the mouse look like it had anxiety.
- 📐 **Live minutes converter** — type your seconds and instantly see it converted to minutes, so you don't have to do maths while pretending to work.
- 🚫 **Max deferred stop: 28800 sec (8 hours)** — that's one full workday, friend. We capped it there because you really don't want to pretend overtime. Nobody fakes staying late voluntarily. Nobody.
